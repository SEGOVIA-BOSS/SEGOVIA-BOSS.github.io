Sensory Processing Disorder (SPD) is complex neurodevelopment disorder that affects millions of people worldwide. It is especially prevalent in non-neurotypical people for example 90 percent of people with the Autistic Spectrum Disorder (ASD) also show some symptoms of SPD.

People with SPD have a broad variability of symptoms, and SPD affects the quality of life of them and their close ones. In general, these symptoms harm their capability to socialize and cope with new environments. Currently, people with SPD only have as solutions to learn coping strategies or to use cumbersome technology not adapted for them.

In Origenes, we would like to bring the state of the art on technology to their service. Advanced electrophysiological and environmental sensors to better understand the needs of assistance on real time. Integrated with actuators to intervene and reduce SPD symptoms. Everything powered by expert systems than learn and adapt to the needs of the individual.

Join us in our mission to revolutionize the way we support individuals with autism. Together, we can create a brighter future for all those affected by this disorder. "TAKE ACTION NOW"

our expertise

Expertise in Medical Technology

Our team at Origenes is primarily composed of highly skilled biomedical engineers who specialize in developing advanced medical technologies for autism rehabilitation. From wearable sensors to neurofeedback systems, we believe that technology can play a critical role in promoting self-regulation, facilitating communication, and enhancing social interactions for individuals with autism.

Data Science and Artificial Intelligence

In addition to our expertise in medical technology, our team is also well-versed in data science and artificial intelligence. We use sophisticated algorithms and machine learning techniques to analyze large datasets and identify patterns that can inform the development of personalized treatment plans. This approach allows us to tailor our interventions to the specific needs of each individual with autism, and continuously improve the effectiveness of our interventions over time.

Multidisciplinary Collaboration

At Origenes, we recognize that successful autism rehabilitation requires a multidisciplinary approach. That's why our engineers work alongside experienced therapists and educators to develop innovative rehabilitation interventions and personalized treatment plans. By collaborating with experts from different fields, we are able to develop comprehensive and effective interventions that address each individual's specific needs and challenges.

Commitment to Research and Innovation

Our team of biomedical engineers is committed to staying up-to-date on the latest research in the field of autism rehabilitation, and actively collaborates with leading researchers and clinicians to incorporate new findings and best practices into our interventions. By staying at the forefront of research and innovation, we are able to continuously improve the effectiveness of our interventions and provide the best possible care to individuals with autism and their families.
